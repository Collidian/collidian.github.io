How it works

From scan to action, in three steps.

Every contact is detected, assessed and classified. You receive only what matters: the real risk and the recommended maneuver.

1 · Detect

Fuses AIS, radar, and RGB and thermal cameras into a single, continuously updated traffic map.

2 · Compute

Estimates the closest point of approach (CPA) and time to CPA (TCPA) for every vessel.

3 · Alert

Classifies the risk as safe, caution or danger and suggests the evasive maneuver in line with COLREG.
